Title: HERMES Individual-Based Modelling Framework
Version: 0.0.1
Description: A lightweight individual-based modelling (IBM) framework for
    simulating infectious disease transmission in structured populations.
    The framework is designed to run as a collection of scripts, facilitating 
    reuse, teaching, and reproducible research.
Author: Lander Willem, University of Antwerp, Belgium
Contact: lander.willem@uantwerpen.be
